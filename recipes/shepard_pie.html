Shepherd's Pie

Description

This is a hearty and comforting recipe for Shepherd's Pie.

Shepherd's Pie is a classic British dish made with a flavorful ground meat filling, typically lamb or beef, topped with a layer of creamy mashed potatoes. The meat filling is cooked with onions, carrots, peas, and a rich gravy, creating a savory base that is perfectly complemented by the smooth and buttery mashed potatoes. Baked until golden and bubbly, Shepherd's Pie is a satisfying and wholesome meal that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add ground lamb or beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, peas, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread the meat mixture evenly in a baking dish. Top with mashed potatoes, spreading them out to cover the meat completely.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.

Enjoy!
